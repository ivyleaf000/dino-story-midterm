Dino Story
By: Aaron, also Niko

Chapter 1
---------
A long long time ago, a ferocious Tyrannosaurus Rex named Teresa lived
next to a rumbling volcano in a swampy, smelly jungle. According to her neighbor, everyone hated Teresa, and according to her neighbor Terry the Pterodactyl, Teresa was the meanest dinosaur in the whole jungle. Teresa spent most of her time stomping through the dense
undergrowth, roaring at smaller dinos that got in her way.

Chapter 2
---------
One day, Teresa saved Terry from certain death because she walked to close to the volcano. They became friends, and everyone lived happily ever after!
